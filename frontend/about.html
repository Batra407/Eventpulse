About EventPulse

Built for Academic Organizers

EventPulse is a full-stack AI-powered platform for collecting structured event feedback, tracking attendance via QR codes, and making data-driven decisions — built exactly for you.

Our Mission

To eliminate the gap between running an event and understanding its impact. We give every academic organizer the tools to collect meaningful feedback, track who showed up, and act on real data — without any technical hassle.

Our Vision

A future where every lecture, seminar, and conference gets better over time — powered by honest participant feedback and intelligent analytics. We envision EventPulse as the defining standard for continuous improvement across universities worldwide.
